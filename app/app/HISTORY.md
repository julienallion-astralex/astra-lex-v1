# Astra Lex — Version History

## Astra Lex 0.1.0 — Origin
**Launch date:** April 12, 2026

Astra Lex 0.1.0 is the original public prototype of a structured space law consultation system.

### Characteristics
- Based on the 1967 Outer Space Treaty
- English language only
- Single-question input
- Short answer output
- Identification of the most relevant article
- Direct article display
- Basic reasoning explanation

### Technical Notes
- Streamlit application
- Rule-based retrieval system
- Single text source
- Immediate response (no multi-step reasoning)

### Status
Public functional prototype.

This version marks the operational origin of Astra Lex.
